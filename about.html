ABOUT ME

My name is Jamie Locatis. After spending twelve years in retail management, I decided it was time for a change. Back to the roots of something I always enjoyed.

I designed my first website in 1999, when I was just 12 years old. I spent years prowling the internet building and running various fansites. Early in college I was offered a fast tracked management career with a retail company I started at in high school. It was a no-brainer. I gained invaluable business skills and leadership experience.

But the time came to move on. I attended Omaha Code School in the Spring of 2017 to get a jumpstart on modern web development practices.

This blog is a place where I can explore and share some of the challenges I have encountered through my development journey.
