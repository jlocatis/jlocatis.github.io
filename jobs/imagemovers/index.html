Hi, I'm Jamie

Former retail manager turned developer. I love learning and building new things --- especially cool websites. Let's chat.

Let's talk about me

I am a web designer.

I am a web designer who loves the internet, marvelous looking gadgets, and generally all things tech. Solving a programming problem or finishing getting that little bit of brilliant css excites me. A well designed website and highly functional program can be a piece of art.

What that means I do.

I find myself entranced by almost anything. I have been told my interests can be all over the place. The world is full of so much to learn and see. I love spending the majority of my actual free time outside, exploring. Whether that means walking around the block, climbing a mountain, or traveling across the world, I simply enjoy the sensation of experiencing something new. I’m also into computers, addicting video games, hockey (let’s see if the Penguins can win the Stanley Cup again!), a bit of photography, and keeping my cat company. Over the years I’ve dabbled in a little bit of everything.

Why I love the things I do.

One of my earliest memories is sitting in front of a Commodore 64, aimlessly pressing the keyboard and watching magic appear on the bright screen in front of me. I have always had a deep fascination with technology, and have always wanted to be a part of the wizardry behind it. I get a warm feeling from finishing projects and helping people. I spent 12 years an retail customer service, 7 of those years in senior management. I can manage projects, people, and most importantly myself. I have an impulse to always be better at what I do. No challenge is ever too much, and never being challenged is ultimately boring.
